About Us

Elicit Engineers & Interiors, founded in 2015 in Gorakhpur, provides Renovation and interior solutions, including design and contracting services for both new projects and renovations. We work closely with trusted partner companies to offer customized interior services, ensuring every client gets the perfect solution for their space.

Our Vision

EE&I was established in Gorakhpur in 2015 to provide comprehensive architectural, building solutions, interior design, HVAC-R, and contracting services for all types of buildings, whether new constructions or refurbishments. To offer a full spectrum of interior services, Elicit collaborates with partner companies, ensuring tailored solutions for clients. We specialize in general contracting, managing projects from greenfield development to final completion. Our expertise includes architectural solutions, construction, MEP, HVAC-R, clean rooms, security & communication systems, and the construction chemical market.
