Frequently Asked Questions

An interior designer helps transform your space by balancing functionality with aesthetics. They bring expert knowledge in layouts, materials, lighting, and design trends ensuring your home looks stunning and works perfectly for your lifestyle. With professional guidance, you avoid costly errors and enjoy a seamless, stress-free experience.
Elicit offers a comprehensive interior design experience from personalized consultations and 3D designs to expert project management and post-installation support. With talented designers, quality craftsmanship, transparent pricing, and reliable timelines, Elicit brings your dream home to life beautifully and effortlessly.
Elicit’s home interior design services cover everything from space planning, modular kitchens, wardrobes, and furniture to wall finishes, ceilings, lighting, flooring, and décor. We also handle electrical, plumbing, and civil work giving you a complete end-to-end solution under one roof.
The cost depends on your home's size, design preferences, and materials. On average, full home interiors can start around ₹3–4 lakh for a 1BHK and increase based on customization and finishes. Elicit offers flexible packages tailored to your budget, with no hidden charges.
Timelines vary based on project scope and home size. Typically, an Elicit project takes 6 to 12 weeks from design finalization to handover. Our process is streamlined to ensure timely execution without compromising on quality or detail.
Here are a few interior design styles that are currently popular:
Minimalist: Simple, clean, and clutter-free designs.
Scandinavian: Light woods, cozy textures, and functional elegance.
Modern Contemporary: Sleek finishes with a blend of comfort and trend.
Industrial: Raw, edgy look with exposed brick and metal.
Boho Chic: Vibrant colors, layered patterns, and artistic accents.
Indian Traditional: Ornate wooden details, ethnic motifs, and warm tones.
Elicit helps you explore these styles and customize them to match your personality and the essence of your home.
